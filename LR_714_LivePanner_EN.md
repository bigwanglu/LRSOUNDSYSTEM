# 7.1.4 LIVE PANNER

7.1.4 LIVE PANNER is an immersive spatial panning tool designed specifically for Ableton Live and the Max for Live environment.

Rather than functioning as a traditional “mixing plugin,” it behaves more like a digital console for real-time spatial performance and sound positioning. The system is built around a 7.1.4 multichannel workflow, allowing users to move sound freely across both horizontal and vertical dimensions while dynamically shaping spatial distribution through real-time Width, Focus, LFE, and XYZ controls.

Unlike traditional studio-oriented software design, 7.1.4 LIVE PANNER emphasizes:

- Real-time performability  
- Spatial intuition  
- Clear and direct control logic  
- Low-friction human interaction  
- The possibility of spatial audio as a playable musical dimension  

The plugin adopts a wide, low-profile layout optimized for Ableton Live’s Device View and is designed specifically for Max for Live’s real-time performance workflow. Rather than attempting to become a large and complex spatial mixing system, it is intended to function more like a playable instrument for immersive sound performance.

# About the Author

7.1.4 LIVE PANNER was designed and developed by Beijing-based musician, producer, sound artist, and live music director Wang Lu (L+R).

His work spans pop music production, experimental electronic music, live performance, immersive multichannel sound systems, and spatial audio design, while continuously exploring the relationship between sound, space, perception, emotional states, and technological systems.

Beyond mainstream music production and large-scale live performances, his recent work has increasingly focused on:

- Immersive spatial sound systems  
- Multichannel live performance  
- Spatial audio instrument design  
- Ambient / healing-oriented sound systems  
- Experimental interactive interfaces  
- AI-assisted music generation and real-time performance tools  

L+R SOUNDSYSTEM is his long-term experimental sound platform and creative system, centered around the idea of treating sound as space rather than simply content.

7.1.4 LIVE PANNER also represents the first systematic exploration by L+R SOUNDSYSTEM into spatial performance control tools.
